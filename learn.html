Budgeting

Budgeting is an essential process that ensures that the money and resources at hand are spent on the activities of an individual depending on the priorities assigned to them. These activities are outlined on an item known as a budget and the amount needed for that activity written along with it.

Savings

Financial independence is the freedom to choose the work you want to be involved in and the amount of hours you want to work. It is also an opportunity to be able to help other people who are not in a position to be of help to other people. Saving an adequate amount of money places you in the position to experience these things and more.

Retirement Plan

Retirement planning is the process of taking steps in ensuring that an individual retires with financial freedom. It involves the task of deciding at what age to retire, where to live after retiring and how much money will be required to cover the expenses of the activities planned for the retirement period.
